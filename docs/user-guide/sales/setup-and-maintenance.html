    Setup & Maintenance

    The Setup & Maintenance menu enlists the following sub-menus:

    Salespersons Salesperson is an employee, agent, consultant, or an independent contractor brining business to your organization.
    Sales Teams A sales team is a group of salespersons having a common name.
    Bonus Slabs for Salespersons Salespersons can be entitled to a bonus, depending upon the amount (slab) of business brought in by them.
    Bonus Slab Details Enter a slab-wise incremental or flat bonus rate (in percentage) which increases as the amount increases.
    Bonus Slab Assignment Assign bonus slabs to salespersons.
    Late Fees Create your custom defined late fees, which can be linked to other documents, such as payment term.
    Payment Terms Define the terms on which you will provide credit to your parties.
    Recurring Invoices Create invoices which automatically recur based on your item, product, or services.
    Recurring Invoices Setup Assign a recurring invoice to a party account.
